About Me

Hi, I'm Saman Hamal, a tech enthusiast from Butwal, Nepal, passionate about coding and exploring new technologies. My journey into programming began with a simple school website assignment, where I first learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Since then, my love for coding has only grown, and I’ve dived deep into frontend web development and languages like C++ and Python. I am particularly interested in artificial intelligence and dream of building an AI voice assistant like Jarvis someday. I enjoy working in teams, leading projects, and connecting with clients on a personal level, ensuring that I not only meet but exceed their expectations. Helping others learn new technologies is something I love doing—it’s rewarding to see others succeed alongside me. While balancing my studies and coding can be a challenge, I’m committed to excelling in both. One of my long-term goals is to participate in the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI) and push my skills to the limit. In my free time, I enjoy playing badminton and working on science projects. When I start something, I make sure to give it my all, right up until I'm too tired to continue. But that’s just the way I like it—immersing myself fully in what I do.
